Privacy Policy for The Journers Travel Agency

Last Updated: 31/08/2023

Introduction

At The Journers Travel Agency, we are committed to safeguarding the privacy of our customers. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website, book our services, or contact us for inquiries. We value your trust and assure you that your data will be kept secure.

Information We Collect

  1. Personal Information: We may collect and process personal information, including but not limited to your name, contact details (email address, phone number, and address), passport information, payment information, and any other information necessary to provide our travel services.
  2. Cookies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience on our website. For more information on how we use cookies, please see our Cookie Policy.

How We Use Your Information

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Booking and Providing Services: To facilitate and manage your travel bookings, process payments, and deliver our travel services.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, requests, and provide customer support.
  • Marketing: To inform you about our special offers, promotions, and updates that may be of interest to you. You may opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements.

Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. We use secure servers, encryption, and access controls to safeguard your data.

Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. However, we may share your information with trusted third parties, such as airlines, hotels, and tour operators, to fulfill your travel bookings and provide you with the best travel experience possible.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request the correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal information when it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.
  • Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw your consent for us to process your personal information at any time.

Cookie Policy

For detailed information on how we use cookies and your choices regarding cookies, please refer to our Cookie Policy.
