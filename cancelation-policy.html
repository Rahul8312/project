Booking, Modification, Cancellation and Payment Policy

Booking Policy:

  • A booking can be done for a maximum of 180 days before the trip start date.
  • A booking can be done for a minimum of 3 hours before the trip start time.
  • Booking can be done through our website at info@thejourners.com, through our mobile app, or calling us at +91-7007069226
  • To confirm the online cab bookings, an advance amount needs to be paid by the customer. The advance amount is based on the trip estimated cost and will be adjusted against the final amount payable at the end of the trip.
  • For every confirmed booking, a car will be allotted at least 8 hours before the trip (2 hours for outstation One-Way). The customer will get an email and/or sms confirmation with the car and driver details after the allotment.
  • Customer should carry a printout of the booking voucher or the booking confirmations SMS for identification.

Note:

  • In case of vehicle accident/break-down the company will provide an alternate vehicle or will refund the advance deposit if booking stands cancelled. Thejourners.com doesn't take any guarantees of any kind while you book the cabs, and any material or immaterial loss due to usage of Thejourners.com shall not be subject to any compensation.
  • In case of no-show by the customer, or no intimation about change of time/date of booking, the booking advance amount will be fully forfeited. In case the customer advance is less than 500 Rs, the company reserves the right to ask for more money to cover losses due to no show/cancellations.
  • In case of a no-show by the driver for a scheduled departure, the company will provide an immediate replacement with a similar car and in case we cannot find a replacement, a full refund of the advance amount will be done.

Booking Modifications:

  • You can modify the start and/or end date (pre-pone or post-pone) for a booking without any modification fee (subject to availability of the vehicle) up to 24 hours or more before the original pickup time.
  • All modification requests should be communicated to us via a phone call to 7007069226 or a mail to email us info@thejourners.com and we will revert with response through email, Call, or SMS.
  • Modifications are allowed even after starting the trip, subject to availability. Such modification will be done entirely at the discretion of Thejourners.com and it may cost extra amount to you.

Remaining Payments after Advance or Payment at Trip Completion:

  • After paying advance amount, the remaining amount for the trip is always paid to the driver. The remaining amount shall be paid to the driver during and at end of the trip. All payments done to the driver will be subject to adjustment against the final bill. Please call Thejourners.com at 7007069226 for any clarifications on billing or other things before/during/end of trip
  • The value of the cash payment to the driver/vendor will be the total cost plus service tax minus the advance payment (if any). The final payment shall always be in cash as the driver might will not be able to charge on card and will not accept cheque payments.
  • In case, the final amount is less than the advance booking deposit (if any), Thejourners.com will refund the excess amount back to the customer after confirming with the driver/operator. This refund is made within 5-7 working days after the completion of the trip.

Cancellation rules For Online Economy(AC) and Economy(Non-AC) Booking:

  • All the cancellation requests should be done via a phone call to 7007069226 or through email at info@thejourners.com
  • In case the booking is cancelled more than 24 hours or more before the pickup time, 75% of the total booking amount will be refunded.
  • In case the booking is cancelled within 24-12 hour before pick up time, 40% of the booking amount will be deducted as penalty.
  • In case the booking is cancelled within 12-6 hour before pick up time, 50% of the booking amount will be deducted as penalty.
  • In case the booking is cancelled within 6 hour before pick up time, 100% of the booking amount will be deducted as penalty.
  • In case the cancellation is done from Thejourners.com end due to unavoidable reasons, 100% of the booking amount will be refunded.
